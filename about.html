What Is The Loom Workshop?

The Loom Workshop is the practical companion to The Lingua Loom. Where The Lingua Loom teaches linguistic theory through content and explanation, The Loom Workshop lets you experience it directly — by typing, analyzing, building, and exploring.

"Every tool here exists because a student somewhere needed to move from knowing about language to actually working with it."

Everything runs entirely in your browser. There is no account to create, no software to install, and no paywall. You can use any tool on any device — phone, tablet, or desktop — and your work stays completely private on your own screen.

Data & Attribution

Some tools in the Workshop fetch live data from open linguistic databases to ensure coverage of the full vocabulary of English, including new and rare words. The sources used are:

  • Wiktionary — etymology, word origin, and historical language data. Operated by the Wikimedia Foundation under CC BY-SA 4.0.
  • Free Dictionary API — definitions and part-of-speech data. A free public API.

Only the specific word being looked up is ever sent in these requests — no other data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
