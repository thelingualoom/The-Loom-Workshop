Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025
Short version: The Loom Workshop does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All tools run entirely inside your browser. Nothing you type is ever sent to our servers — because we do not have any.

Who We Are

The Loom Workshop is a free, browser-based linguistics toolkit created by The Lingua Loom. It is an educational resource designed for linguistics students, educators, and language enthusiasts.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, process, or transmit any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, or any identifying information
  • The text, words, or sentences you enter into any tool
  • Your location or IP address
  • Browser fingerprints, device information, or usage patterns
  • Cookies for tracking or analytics purposes

How the Tools Work

Every tool on this site runs entirely within your browser using client-side JavaScript. When you type a word or sentence, the processing happens locally on your device. Your input never leaves your browser to reach our servers.

Third-Party APIs

Some tools fetch data from external linguistic databases to provide up-to-date information on word origins, definitions, and grammatical categories. When this happens:

  • Only the specific word you look up is sent in the request — nothing else
  • No identifying information about you is included
  • These requests go to publicly accessible APIs that operate under their own privacy policies

The external services used are:

  • Wiktionary API (en.wiktionary.org) — for etymology and word origin data. Operated by the Wikimedia Foundation. Data is available under CC BY-SA 4.0.
  • Free Dictionary API (api.dictionaryapi.dev) — for word definitions and part-of-speech data. A free, open public API.

Hosting

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect standard server logs (such as IP addresses) as part of their infrastructure. This is outside our control. You can read GitHub's privacy policy at github.com.

Children

The Loom Workshop is an educational resource suitable for all ages. Because we collect no personal data whatsoever, there are no special concerns regarding use by children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the date at the top of this page will be revised. Since we collect no data, any changes are unlikely to affect you in any meaningful way.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or the tools on this site, you can reach us through The Lingua Loom.
